hotair, a jamBOX game.

code: michael carriere
art: elliott mitchell and ryan chadwick
music: jared burrell

description: you are a balloon. click to read a quote, and fill your balloon with hot air. the more you fill your balloon, the higher it will go. over time, if you stop reading, the balloon will land on the ground. the time in the game mimics the time in the real world.
